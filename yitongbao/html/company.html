上海易通电子商务有限公司   中文 | English
Etong E-commerce (Shanghai)

上海易通电子商务有限公司（简称上海易通）是全亚洲规模最大的大宗商品经纪服务提供商，业务主要覆盖能源化工行业，年交易量达5000万吨，占流通市场约30%的份额，已服务超过5000家客户。我们的客户是全球范围内的能源和化工生产商、贸易商、下游制造企业等各垂直行业参与者，以及投资公司、资管公司、券商及投行。

上海易通既是上海清算所大宗商品金融衍生品授权经纪公司（清算所ID 3015），也是新加坡交易所IDB（经纪商编码 S611）。截至目前，上海易通的成交量占上海清算所和新交所化工品掉期交易6成以上。鉴于上海易通在大宗商品金融衍生品交易上的优异表现，近几年先后获得由上海清算所颁发的“大宗商品清算经纪公司优秀奖”；浦发银行颁发的“开户贡献奖”、“市场流动性贡献奖”、“业务品种交易奖”、“创新争先奖”、“综合贡献奖”和“年度贡献奖”；以及新交所颁发的“最活跃MEG经纪商奖”。

目前易通撮合业务涉及现货交易、上清所掉期、新交所SWAP和自贸区化工四个板块，主要交易乙二醇、甲醇、苯乙烯、纯苯、甲苯和二甲苯等产品。

Etong E-commerce (Shanghai) is a professional and leading bulk commodity brokerage service provider in Asia. Its business mainly based on energy and chemicals sectors, with over 50 million tons annual trading volume. Etong also occupied about 30% chemicals trading market share and serivced over 5,000 clients till now. Etong’s clients cover producers, traders and downstream manufacturers among the global energy and chemical market, also with the investment companies, capital management companies, securities companies and investment banks.

Etong is the certified bulk commodity brokers both by Shanghai Clearing House (ID: 3015) and SGX (IDB code S611). So far, Etong accounted more than 60% of the turnover in Shanghai Clearing House and SGX chemical swap. In view of the outstanding performance of Etong, it had successive acquisition the “The Excellent Broker Award in Commodities Clearing” by Shanghai Clearing House, the “Most Active MEG Inter-dealer Broker 2017” by SGX, “Market fluidity contribution Award” “Comprehensive contribution Award” ,etc. by SPD Bank.

Currently, Etong's brokerage involves four plates, which are spots, SICX, swaps in Shanghai Clearing House and SGX，it mainly focused on MEG, MEOH, SM, BZ, Toluene and MX.
